About Us

Marine Data Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a South African company owned by Staff and the Kongsberg Group of Norway. The head office of Marine Data Solutions is based in Cape Town with national points of presence in Durban and Port Elizabeth. The planned international offices form an integral part of our future objectives and will be realised while navigating our future.

Marine Data Solutions is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company and has a Level 1, BBBEE certification.

The African Continent remains a primary focus region for Marine Data Solutions. Our association with the Kongsberg Group allows Marine Data Solutions and its customers access to the latest world class technologies. Our Customers and Staff are the two most important parts of our business
